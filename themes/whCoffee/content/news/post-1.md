Mark your calendar for March 20, 2023, as {{< site-title >}} presents a special event dedicated to the art of traditional coffee brewing. This event will immerse participants in the rich history of coffee-making, from the ancient methods of brewing to the tools and techniques used to perfect the craft. It’s not just about brewing a cup of coffee; it’s about understanding the heritage and craftsmanship behind every sip.

During the event, you’ll have the opportunity to learn hands-on brewing methods from experienced baristas and coffee connoisseurs. Whether it’s mastering the pour-over technique, exploring the traditional Javanese coffee brewing style, or learning how to use manual coffee tools, this event is designed for both beginners and seasoned coffee enthusiasts. Participants will also get to savor some of the finest, locally sourced coffee blends while enjoying a warm, community atmosphere.

As part of our mission to foster connections over coffee, this event will also include a discussion session where attendees can share their coffee stories and experiences. It’s the perfect opportunity to engage with fellow coffee lovers, exchange ideas, and deepen your appreciation for the world of coffee. We look forward to welcoming you to this enriching and flavorful journey!
