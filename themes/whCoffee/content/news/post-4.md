Unveiling the Richness of Single-Origin Coffee at SeduhRasa

Mark your calendars for an exciting journey into the world of single-origin coffee, where we’ll explore how geography, climate, and cultivation techniques come together to create distinctive flavors. This interactive workshop, hosted by SeduhRasa, will be led by our expert baristas who will take you through the diverse coffee-growing regions of Indonesia. From the volcanic soils of Sumatra to the highlands of Sulawesi, each region contributes unique qualities to the beans it produces.

Throughout the session, you’ll get the chance to taste and compare various single-origin coffees, each with its own story and background. Our baristas will share insights into how these beans are carefully sourced from local farmers, who take pride in maintaining sustainable and traditional farming methods. By understanding the origins of each cup, you’ll gain a deeper appreciation for the craftsmanship that goes into producing high-quality coffee.

A key focus of the workshop will be on the concept of terroir, which refers to the environmental factors that affect the flavor of coffee beans. Just like wine, coffee beans absorb characteristics from the region they’re grown in, including soil composition, altitude, and climate. During the event, we’ll explain how these elements impact the taste, aroma, and texture of each brew, allowing you to identify the subtle nuances in your cup.

Whether you’re a coffee enthusiast or a casual drinker, this workshop offers something for everyone. Not only will you leave with a better understanding of single-origin coffee, but you’ll also be able to refine your palate and recognize the unique characteristics of beans from different regions. Don’t miss this opportunity to elevate your coffee knowledge and discover the world of flavors waiting to be explored.
